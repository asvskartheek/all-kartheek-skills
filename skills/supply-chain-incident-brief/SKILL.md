# Supply Chain Incident Brief

Use this skill for fast, factual incident communication.

## Output sections

### What happened
### Affected versions / blast radius
### Indicators of compromise / behaviors
### Immediate remediation steps
### Verification steps
### Suggested public/internal comms

## Rules

- Separate confirmed facts from allegations.
- Keep remediation concrete.
- If asked for marketing copy, keep it clearly downstream of the incident facts.
- Don’t invent compromise mechanics not backed by sources or artifacts.
